Cancellation Policy:

  1. 1. All ticket cancellation requests must be submitted through the official Nabil Paribahan website or designated ticket counters.
  2. 2. Refunds will only be provided for cancellations made within the specified time frame. Cancellations made 48 hours or more before departure will receive a full refund (excluding service charges), cancellations made 24 to 48 hours before departure will receive a 50% refund (excluding service charges), and cancellations made less than 24 hours before departure are non-refundable.
  3. 3. Tickets purchased under promotional offers, discounts, or last-minute bookings are strictly non-refundable.
  4. 4. A nominal service charge will be deducted from all refunds to cover administrative costs.
  5. 5. If a passenger fails to board the bus at the scheduled departure time, the ticket will be forfeited, and no refund will be issued.
  6. 6.Partial cancellations for bookings with multiple seats are allowed and will follow the same refund rules for each canceled seat.
  7. 7. Refunds will be processed through the original payment method and may take 7-10 business days to reflect in the account.
  8. 8. In cases of unforeseen circumstances, such as natural disasters or government restrictions, Nabil Paribahan reserves the right to offer refunds or reschedule tickets at its discretion.
  9. 9. This cancellation policy is subject to change at any time without prior notice. Updates will be communicated through the official website.
  10. 10. For questions or assistance regarding cancellations, contact our support team via the Contact Us page or helpline.
